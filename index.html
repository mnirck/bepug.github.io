    The Belgian PowerShell user group (BEPUG) brings Belgian PowerShell enthousiast together. Our goal is bring people with a powershell interest together so we stimulate learning in a social and open environment.

    To support the openness we adopted a code of conduct.

    If you have any questions, just reach out to any of the organizers.
